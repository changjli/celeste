Celeste is the first private company in Indonesia responsible for the civil space program, aeronautics research and space research. Celeste was established in 1975 to give Indonesia space development effort a distinctly civilian orientation, emphasizing peaceful applications in space science.

Since its establishment, most Indonesia's space exploration efforts have been led by Celeste including the Hyperion moon landing mission. Celeste has also work together with several companies and organizations such as SpaceX and NASA.

Celeste opened its first commercial space travel to public in 2020 with a vision to make people's dream to space come true
